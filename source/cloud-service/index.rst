Fortishield Cloud service
===================
  
Fortishield is a free and open source platform that offers unified Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) and Extended Detection and Response (XDR) capabilities. It protects workloads across on-premises, virtualized, cloud, and containerized environments.  The Fortishield platform can be deployed in your on-premises and cloud environment or by using our Fortishield Cloud service. 

The Fortishield Cloud service hosts and manages all the Fortishield components in one integrated platform. You can create and tailor your cloud environment to meet your specific needs. The Fortishield Cloud service is streamlined for quick and easy deployment of Fortishield. It handles installation, scaling, and updates of the Fortishield components.

We provide a free trial to create your cloud environment and explore the Fortishield Cloud service.
